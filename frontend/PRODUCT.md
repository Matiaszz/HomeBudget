# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Individuals and families managing personal monthly budgets. They need to track expenses and income to regain control of their finances, reduce money-related stress, and plan for their future. The primary context is simple daily or weekly checks on mobile or desktop devices.

## Product Purpose
HomeBudget provides a clean, secure frontend to authenticate, check profile data, validate user age, and manage budget entries. Success is achieved when a user can understand their financial health and log data in seconds.

## Positioning
A straightforward, secure home budgeting tool that adapts permissions based on user age.

## Brand Personality
Trustworthy, clean, and friendly. The interface reduces financial stress by focusing on visual clarity, calm color tones, and positive reinforcement.

## Anti-references
Cluttered legacy financial portals with confusing navigation, overwhelming graphs, and hard-to-read small fonts.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity**: Minimize visual noise; only show the information that matters most.
- **Age-Appropriate Adaptability**: Tailor capabilities and messaging seamlessly based on user properties (such as age verification).
- **Stress-Free Interactions**: Design forms, feedbacks, and status indicators to feel encouraging rather than daunting.
- **Frictionless Input**: Make budget logging and configuration take as few taps or clicks as possible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA baseline with high contrast text, fully keyboard-navigable interactive controls, distinct focus rings, and fully responsive layouts that perform well on mobile web viewports.
